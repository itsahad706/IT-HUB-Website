About IT HUB

IT HUB is an educational technology platform dedicated to providing high-quality Computer Science, Programming, and Networking education in the Pashto language.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make IT education accessible, practical, and affordable for Pashto-speaking students across Pakistan, Afghanistan, and the global Pashtun community. We believe language should never be a barrier to learning modern technology.

Meet the Team
